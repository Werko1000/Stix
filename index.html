Art that lives
on the chain.

Most NFT projects store images on IPFS or a centralized server. If the team disappears, the art disappears. Pixl is different.

Every pixel, every color, every stick position is encoded directly into the smart contract on Base. The SVG is generated on-chain, on demand, every time. No image to host. Nothing to lose.

Burn two Pixl together and get all elements in one canvas — yours to arrange however you like. Keep burning, keep building. The chain remembers everything.

How it works
01 — Mint

Random elements, VRF-powered.

Each Pixl is minted with random sticks and Tetrominos — color, length, type all chosen by Chainlink VRF. No two are alike.

02 — Burn

Burn two, get one richer.

Sacrifice two Pixl and receive one carrying all elements. Burn again. Stack more. The more you burn, the richer your canvas.

03 — Create

Arrange. Commit. Own.

Use the on-chain editor to place every element exactly where you want. Save to the contract. That's your art, permanently.
