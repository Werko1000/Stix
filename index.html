Art that lives
on the chain.

Most NFT projects store their images on IPFS or a centralized server. If the team disappears, the art disappears. Stix is different.

Every pixel, every color, every stick position is encoded directly into the smart contract on Base. The SVG is generated on-chain, on demand, every time. There is no image to host. There is nothing to lose.

When you burn two Stix together, you get all six sticks in one canvas — yours to arrange however you like. Keep burning, keep building. The chain remembers everything.

How it works
01 — Mint

You get three sticks.

Each Stix is minted with three randomly generated pixel lines — a color from the 16-color palette, a length between 5 and 15 pixels, and one of four directions.

02 — Burn

Burn two, get one.

Sacrifice two Stix and receive a single new one carrying all six sticks. Burn again. Stack more. The more you burn, the richer your canvas becomes.

03 — Create

Arrange. Commit. Own.

Use the on-chain editor to place every stick exactly where you want it. Save the arrangement to the contract. That’s your art, permanently.
